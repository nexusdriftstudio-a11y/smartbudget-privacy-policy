Privacy Policy for SmartBudget Pro

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to SmartBudget Pro ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application, SmartBudget AI (the "App").

This policy is designed to be compliant with major privacy regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union, Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), laws in the USA, and the legislation of Ukraine.

1. No Data Collection by Developer

We, the developers of SmartBudget Pro, do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or financial data.

SmartBudget Pro is a fully offline application. All data you enter into the App is stored exclusively on your local device in an encrypted database. We have no access to this information.

2. How Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored in a secure, encrypted SQLite database directly on your mobile device. The encryption key is managed by you, ensuring that only you can access your data. Since the data never leaves your device, it is not shared with us or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Our App uses third-party services that may collect information. Below is a list of these services and links to their privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect anonymous data to serve personalized ads. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: Used to provide an optional authentication method. When you use these services, we only receive your basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) for account creation. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Please review the privacy policies of Google and Apple.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

4. Your Rights

You have full control over your data directly within the App. You can access, edit, delete, and export (via the backup feature) your data at any time.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our app store listing.
