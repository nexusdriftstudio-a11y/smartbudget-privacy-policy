Account and Data Deletion for SmartBudget Pro

Developer: NexusDrift Studio

This page provides instructions on how to delete your account and all associated data from the SmartBudget AI application.

Important: SmartBudget Pro is an offline application. All your financial data is stored exclusively on your local device. We, the developers, do not have access to it. Therefore, the deletion process is performed by you directly within the app.

How to Delete Your Account and Data

To permanently delete your account and all related data (including transactions, budgets, wallets, etc.), please follow these steps inside the app:

  1. Go to the "Settings" screen.
  2. Navigate to the "Profile" section.
  3. Tap the "Delete Account" button.
  4. Confirm your decision in the confirmation dialog.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon confirming the deletion, the following data will be permanently and irretrievably removed from your device:

  • Your user profile (name, email).
  • All transactions, categories, and budgets.
  • All wallets and their balances.
  • All debt and loan records.
  • All application settings tied to your account.

There is no retention period; the data is deleted immediately.
